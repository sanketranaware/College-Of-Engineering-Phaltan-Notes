📋 How to upload and share your file

  1. 1 Click "Open Google Drive" below. Sign in with your college Google account if asked.
  2. 2 Click "+ New" → "File Upload" and select your PDF or notes file.
  3. 3 Once uploaded, right-click the file → click "Share".
  4. 4 Under "General access", change Restricted → Anyone with the link.
  5. 5 Set the role to Viewer (students can view, not edit).
  6. 6 Click "Copy link" and paste it in the field below.
⚠️ You must set access to "Anyone with the link" before pasting. If you skip this step, students will see an "Access Denied" error.
