Privacy Policy

Welcome to notes.sanketranaware.in. Your privacy is important to us. This website provides educational study materials uploaded by teachers for students to access freely.

1. Introduction

The purpose of this website is to provide easy access to academic notes. Teachers can upload study materials and students can download them without creating an account.

2. Information We Collect

We collect limited information required for website functionality.

  • Teacher name
  • Email address
  • Password for login authentication

Students can download notes without providing personal information.

3. How We Use Information

  • To manage teacher accounts
  • To allow teachers to upload notes
  • To maintain website functionality
  • To improve the platform

4. Teacher Accounts and Login Data

Only teachers can create accounts. Login credentials are used only for authentication and managing uploaded notes. Teachers are responsible for protecting their login information.

5. File Upload and Storage

Teachers may upload educational materials such as PDFs and documents. These files are stored on the server and are made available to students for learning purposes.

6. Cookies and Analytics

Basic cookies may be used to improve website functionality and understand general usage patterns.

7. Third-Party Services

The website may use hosting services or analytics tools to maintain performance and reliability.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored information and prevent unauthorized access.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when necessary. Changes will be reflected on this page.

10. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact the website developer.
